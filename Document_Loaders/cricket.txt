On a sunlit field where the green grass gleams,
Echo the cheers like childhood dreams.
The bat swings wide with perfect grace,
As the ball flies fast in a thrilling race.

The bowler runs with focus tight,
Delivering speed with all his might.
The crowd holds breath, then roars out loud,
As victory stands tall and proud.

From dusty streets to grand stadium light,
Cricket unites hearts in pure delight.
